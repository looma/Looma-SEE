  FOUND [English] QNone: Proof:
Let the mass of the Earth be '$M$' and its radius be '$R$'. An object of mass '$m$' is on its surface.
Gravitational force, $F = \frac{GMm}{R^2}$ ... (i)
According to Newton's second law, $F = mg$ ... (ii)
From equations (i) and (ii), $mg = \frac{GMm}{R^2}$
Therefore, $g = \frac{GM}{R^2}$
Here, $G$ and $M$ are constants, so $g \propto \frac{1}{R^2}$. 

Variation on Earth:
The Earth is not perfectly round; it is flattened at the poles and bulged at the equator. 
Therefore, the radius ($R$) is less at the poles and more at the equator. Since $g \propto \frac{1}{R^2}$, the value of '$g$' is more at the poles ($9.83\text{ m/s}^2$) and less at the equator ($9.78\text{ m/s}^2$).

Excessive use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides degrades soil quality and destroys soil microorganisms. These chemicals mix with rainwater and reach water sources, causing water pollution and harming aquatic life. Entering the food chain, they negatively affect human health, causing serious diseases like cancer.

Management Measures:
* Use of Organic Fertilizers: Using compost manure instead of chemical fertilizers.
* Integrated Pest Management (IPM): Adopting biological and local methods instead of chemical pesticides.
* Awareness: Educating farmers about the proper use of fertilizers/pesticides and their harmful effects.
* Regulation: Government should ban and regulate the use of harmful pesticides.
